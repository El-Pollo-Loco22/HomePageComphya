About Comphya

Our focus is to bring a better and comfortable therapy for erectile dysfunction to patients non‑amendable to oral drugs. Comphya is a Swiss medical device company, spin‑off from the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), specialized in the treatment of erectile dysfunction to patients non‑responsive to oral therapy.

Our Mission

Innovation Based on Neuroprosthetics Principles

With an innovation based on neuroprosthetics principles, our mission is to offer a unique and superior treatment for erectile dysfunction. We aim to deliver a comfortable therapy alternative for patients who do not respond to oral medications.

Who We Are

Founded as a spin‑off from EPFL, Comphya brings together experience in biomedical engineering, clinical research, and medical device development to translate science into meaningful patient outcomes.
