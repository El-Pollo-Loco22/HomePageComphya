About Comphya

Our focus is to deliver an effective, patient-friendly therapy for erectile dysfunction to individuals who require alternatives to oral medication. Comphya is a Swiss medical device company, spun out of the Swiss Federal Technology Institute of Lausanne (EPFL), dedicated to advancing treatment for erectile dysfunction.

Innovation

Based on neuromodulation principles to restore natural nerve function

CaverSTIM™ applies advanced neuromodulation technology to the delicate cavernous nerves responsible for erectile function. By delivering targeted stimulation during and after surgery, the system aims to help preserve nerve health and support recovery during the critical healing window following radical prostatectomy.

Patient-Focused

Comfortable, drug-free therapy for men unresponsive to oral medications

For men who do not respond to oral therapies, current options can be invasive or unsatisfying. CaverSTIM™ offers a new path — designed to fit within standard surgical procedures and provide discreet, ongoing support for nerve recovery and erectile function.

Research-Driven

EPFL spin-off translating science into meaningful outcomes for men's health

Founded at one of Europe's leading centers for innovation, Comphya unites expertise in biomedical engineering, neuroprosthetics, and clinical research. Our multidisciplinary team is advancing scientific discovery into a therapy that may redefine post-surgical recovery for men worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve men's well-being by addressing nerve-related erectile challenges with Comphya's innovative technology, CaverSTIM™. Through precision neuromodulation, we aim to support natural function recovery for men affected by prostate cancer and other urologic procedures, helping restore confidence, intimacy, and quality of life.

Who We Are

Founded as a spin-off from EPFL, Comphya brings together experts in medical device development, urology, and neuroengineering to pioneer new standards in post-surgical care. With a foundation in rigorous research and patient-focused innovation, we are driven to deliver meaningful change for men affected by erectile dysfunction worldwide.
